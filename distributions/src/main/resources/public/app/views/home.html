Prime Pad Industrial Corporation Pampanga is the official distributor of Sealed Air - Diversey Care products in Pampanga.

Prime Pad Industrial Corporation is a corporation duly registered under Philippine laws with the Securities and Exchange Commission on June 04, 2008.

Our company as shown in our primary purpose committed to engage in the business of buying, selling, marketing, distribution of goods at wholesale/retail as may be permitted by law.

Primepad's mission is to be one of the most trustworthy independent distributor possessed with selling competence, honesty and good business reputation.

We opted to sell/offer products worthy of our needs. We sell products that would help in the maintenance of cleanliness inside and outside our homes. We meticulously chose products which are proven to be highly effective against a wide variety of pathogenic microorganisms including bacteria

To attain our purpose, we purposely got the trust and confidence of Diversey which was earlier called JohnsonDiversey Philippines, Inc. Its products were being chosen by noted establishments in our country and majority of public users.

We have a variety of cleaning products suited to your purpose. We sell waxes, cleaners, floor polishers and accessories and other allied products regularly manufactured originally packed and marketed by the said company.

We sought first all the legal requirements like registration as a corporation, together with permits and certifications to be able to deal with the public legally.

The Board of Directors of PRIMEPAD INDUSTRIAL CORPORATION fully supports the company's commitment to continue to serve you for as long as you patronize the products it offers.

I assure you, YOU CAN RELY ON US!
