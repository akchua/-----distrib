Dermbliss Corporation is a corporation duly registered under Philippine laws with the Securities and Exchange Commission on March 2020 mainly for the purpose of buying and selling, marketing, distributing as wholesale/retail of goods specifically cleaning and sanitation items.

The company's main office is at 42 Buenos Aires St. Vista Verde North Brgy. 166 Caloocan City, Third District, NCR, Philippines, 1420. The telephone number is: 02-798 78591.

The company is headed by Alicia Marie Concepcion Malasa as the president. Aimee Pineda Chua, is the VP-COO, Alvin A. Pineda and Lexidy Agustin Sanchez are the VP-Admin whileFerdinand Agustin Sanchez, Jr. is VP-CFO.
